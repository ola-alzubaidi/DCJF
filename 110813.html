From the
 PRESIDENT
CHARLES FISHMAN
     
2014 will be a milestone year for the DC Jazz Festival as we will be celebrating our 10th anniversary and are very excited about the program we're preparing.

We're in the process of finalizing another outstanding series of concerts, club performances, and family programs, at our usual venues - The Hamilton Live, Bohemian Caverns, the National Gallery of Art Sculpture Garden, Sixth & I Historic Synagogue, the Kennedy Center's Millennium Stage, as well as new venues such as the National Theatre and two fantastic outdoor concerts at the beautiful Capital Riverfront...

From the
 EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR
SUNNY SUMTER
     
As the holiday season begins, we extend jazzy warm wishes and holiday cheer across the DC region and beyond. We love jazz and recognize the power that the performing arts has to transform lives and are especially committed to getting young people "jazzed" about learning, through our dynamic education initiatives! They are our future performers and patrons, the next generation of jazz lovers! Education is truly my greatest commitment to this organization that I serve so proudly.

Give the gift of jazz this holiday season and the gift of music to the many children we serve. The Roberta Flack Music Education Program enriches the lives of thousands of DC public and charter school students with exciting collaborative educational programs year-round like our Jazzin' at Sitar afterschool initiative at Sitar Arts Center, our Jazz for a Healthier Generation initiative at Children's National Medical Center, and our DC Jazz Bops literacy program at THEARC, among others...
